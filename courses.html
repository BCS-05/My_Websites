Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and enrollment process

Do I need prior programming experience?

No, most of our courses are designed for complete beginners. We start from the very basics and gradually build up to advanced concepts. Our beginner-friendly curriculum includes plenty of hands-on exercises and support.

What is the course duration and time commitment?

Courses range from 8-12 weeks. We recommend dedicating 10-15 hours per week for optimal learning. However, you can learn at your own pace as all materials are available 24/7.

What kind of certificate will I receive?

You'll receive a verified digital certificate upon completion, which is recognized by industry partners. The certificate includes your name, course details, and can be shared on LinkedIn.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans including monthly installments. Contact our admissions team to discuss options that work for you.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 mentor support, weekly live Q&A sessions, dedicated Slack community, code reviews, and career counseling services.
