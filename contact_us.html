Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about courses and admissions

What programming languages do you teach?

We offer comprehensive courses in Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, and web development technologies including HTML/CSS, React, Node.js, and modern frameworks. Our curriculum is regularly updated to include the latest industry trends.

Do I need prior programming experience?

No prior experience is required! We offer beginner-friendly courses that start from the absolute basics. Our experienced instructors guide you step-by-step, ensuring you build a strong foundation in programming.

What is the duration of your courses?

Course durations vary depending on the program. Short intensive courses run for 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive programs can take 3-6 months. We also offer flexible self-paced learning options for busy schedules.

Do you offer career support?

Yes! We provide extensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, portfolio development, and job placement assistance. Our industry partnerships help connect graduates with tech opportunities.

Can I get a certificate after completion?

Absolutely! Upon successful completion of any course, you'll receive a certified digital certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and other professional platforms. Our certificates are recognized by industry partners.
